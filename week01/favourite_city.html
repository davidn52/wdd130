My Favourite City

My favourite city is St. Petersburg,it's often referred to as the 'Venice of the North,' it is a stunning city renowned for its imperial history and breathtaking architecture. Founded by Tsar Peter the Great in 1703, it boasts magnificent landmarks such as the Hermitage Museum, the Winter Palace, and the iconic Church of the Savior on Spilled Blood. With its picturesque canals and vibrant cultural scene, St. Petersburg is a captivating blend of art, history, and beauty, making it a must-visit destination in Russia
