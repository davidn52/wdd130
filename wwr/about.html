At White Water Rafting, we create safe and unforgettable river adventures that connect people with nature. We believe every rapid is a chance for courage and teamwork, and we are committed to guiding every guest with skill, respect, and passion. Where adventure flows

History

White Water Rafting was founded in 2020 by David Okebe Nwojo with a passion for adventure and a deep respect for the river. What began as a small team of outdoor enthusiasts offering guided trips quickly grew into a trusted rafting company known for safety, skill, and unforgettable experiences. Over the years, we have expanded our routes, trained expert guides, and welcomed thousands of adventurers eager to explore the thrill and beauty of the water.
