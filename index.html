Abia State, Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant and diverse country located in West Africa, known as the most populous nation on the continent. It is rich in culture, history, and natural resources, with over 250 ethnic groups and more than 500 languages spoken. The country is famous for its lively music, film industry (Nollywood), and delicious cuisine. Major cities like Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt showcase Nigeria’s growing economy and urban energy, while its landscapes range from tropical rainforests and savannas to the Niger River and Atlantic coastline. Nigeria is often called the “Giant of Africa” for its influence across politics, culture, and business.
